# Problem Card — AM Commission & Dispute Process

> One card per named problem. Cards get richer over time — never start fresh, always append.

---

## Problem statement

AMs have no reliable, transparent way to verify their commission calculations, dispute errors, or track resolution — because tracking is manual, rules change without notice, dispute windows are unreasonably short (2 hours), there is no dedicated dispute handler, and pending disputes simply expire. The result is that AMs lose pay for work they legitimately completed, spend hours manually tracking to protect themselves, and experience significant stress and anxiety around commission week.
